Our Story

Founded in 1995, Hassan Abbas Restaurant brings the authentic taste of Middle Eastern cuisine to your table. Our founder, Hassan Abbas, started this restaurant with a simple mission: to share the flavors of his homeland with the world.

Using only the freshest ingredients and traditional cooking methods, we prepare each dish with the same care and attention as we would for our own family. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, ensuring an authentic dining experience.

Whether you're craving our famous hummus, succulent kebabs, or our signature baklava, every bite tells a story of tradition, passion, and culinary excellence.
